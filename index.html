Opt-In to Receipt Confirmation SMS

To use the receipt submission service, text the keyword RECEIPTS to the number below:

+1 (855) 552-2331

By texting this number, you consent to receive automated transactional text messages confirming your receipt submissions. Message frequency varies based on your submissions. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

What is this service?

Phoenix AI Consulting operates an automated expense receipt processing system on behalf of its business clients. Authorized employees submit photos of receipts via text message (SMS/MMS) to the toll-free number above. The system processes the receipt and sends a confirmation reply with the extracted details (vendor, amount, date).

How enrollment works

Employees are enrolled by their employer as part of the company's expense reimbursement program. The employer provides a list of authorized employee phone numbers to Phoenix AI Consulting. Only messages from registered phone numbers are processed.

To opt in:

  • Your employer registers your phone number with the service
  • You text RECEIPTS to +1 (855) 552-2331 to confirm your consent
  • You may then begin submitting receipt photos via text message

What messages will I receive?

You will only receive replies in direct response to your submissions. Messages include:

  • Confirmation of receipt submission (vendor, amount, date)
  • Requests to resubmit if the image is unclear
  • Notification if your phone number is not registered

No marketing, promotional, or unsolicited messages are ever sent. Message frequency depends entirely on how often you submit receipts. Typical usage is 1-10 messages per month.

How to opt out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from +1 (855) 552-2331
  • Contacting your employer to be removed from the program
  • Emailing support@phoenixaiconsulting.com

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You can opt back in at any time by texting RECEIPTS to the number above.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message from this number, or contact:

  • Email: support@phoenixaiconsulting.com
  • Phone: Contact your employer's HR or finance department

Message & data rates

Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. Phoenix AI Consulting does not charge for this service. Supported carriers include all major US carriers.
