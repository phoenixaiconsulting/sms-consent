SMS Consent & Disclosure

Phoenix AI Consulting — Automated Receipt Confirmation Service

What is this service?

Phoenix AI Consulting operates an automated expense receipt processing system on behalf of its business clients. Authorized employees submit photos of receipts via text message (SMS/MMS) to a designated toll-free number. The system processes the receipt and sends a confirmation reply.

How enrollment works

Employees are enrolled by their employer as part of the company's expense reimbursement program. The employer provides a list of authorized employee phone numbers. Only messages from registered phone numbers are processed.

By texting a receipt photo to the designated number, you consent to receiving transactional reply messages confirming your submission.

What messages will I receive?

You will only receive replies in direct response to your submissions. Messages include:

  • Confirmation of receipt submission (vendor, amount, date)
  • Requests to resubmit if the image is unclear
  • Notification if your phone number is not registered

No marketing, promotional, or unsolicited messages are ever sent. Message frequency depends entirely on how often you submit receipts.

How to opt out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from this number
  • Contacting your employer to be removed from the program
  • Emailing support@phoenixaiconsulting.com

Message & data rates

Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. Phoenix AI Consulting does not charge for this service.

Privacy

Phone numbers and receipt data are stored securely and used solely for expense reimbursement processing. Data is not shared with third parties beyond the employer who enrolled you. Receipt images and extracted data are retained only for the duration needed to complete the reimbursement cycle.
