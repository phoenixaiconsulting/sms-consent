SMS Consent & Opt-In

Gage Platform — Business Operations Alerts, operated by Phoenix AI Consulting

What this messaging program is

The Gage Platform is internal business-operations software operated by Phoenix AI Consulting for Gage Consulting, a trucking and hauling logistics company. This SMS program sends work-related operational notifications to a small, closed group of recipients: the owners and administrators of Gage Consulting, and its employed job-site managers. It never messages the general public or Gage Consulting's customers.

Messages include: quote documents ready for an administrator's review or approval, confirmations of approvals, secure sign-in links for assigned job-site managers, and reminders about missing daily job reports. Administrators typically initiate conversations themselves by texting the platform first (for example, sending a voice memo describing a quote to prepare).

How every recipient opts in

  1. Administrators (owners/executives of Gage Consulting): enrolled in person during platform onboarding. Each administrator is shown this consent page, agrees to receive operational alerts, and is registered by name and mobile number in the platform. Administrators also initiate most conversations themselves by texting the platform's number first.
  2. Job-site managers (employees of Gage Consulting): when the business owner assigns an employee as the manager of a job, the owner informs them of the assignment and obtains their consent to receive work notifications; the employee's number is then registered in the platform by the owner.

Immediately after enrollment, every recipient receives a one-time confirmation message stating the program name, that message and data rates may apply, that message frequency varies, and how to get help or stop:

"You're enrolled in Gage Consulting operations alerts (quotes, job reports, sign-in links). Msg&data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply HELP for help, STOP to stop."

Consent is never a precondition of purchase (recipients are business principals and employees, not consumers), phone numbers are collected directly from the individuals themselves, and consent records (who enrolled, when, and by whom) are retained in the platform's audit log.

Message frequency, rates, and your choices

  • Message frequency varies with business activity (typically a few messages per week per recipient).
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation of your opt-out and nothing further. Reply START to re-subscribe.
  • Reply HELP for assistance at any time.
  • Your mobile number and opt-in consent are never shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
