Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Phoenix AI Consulting ("we," "us," "our") operates an automated receipt processing service via SMS/MMS on behalf of business clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our SMS-based receipt submission service (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we collect:

  • Phone number — Your mobile phone number, provided by your employer when enrolling you in the Service
  • SMS/MMS content — Text messages and images (receipt photos) you send to our toll-free number
  • Extracted receipt data — Vendor name, transaction amount, date, line items, and payment method as extracted from your receipt images
  • Employee name and department — As provided by your employer in the enrollment roster
  • Message metadata — Timestamps, message delivery status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Process and extract data from submitted receipt images
  • Store receipt records for your employer's expense reimbursement program
  • Send you transactional confirmation messages about your submissions
  • Identify you as an authorized user of the Service

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the receipt processing service.

4. Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Your employer — The business client who enrolled you, for expense reimbursement processing
  • Service providers — Cloud infrastructure providers (data storage, AI processing) that help us operate the Service, under strict data processing agreements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your phone number or opt-in consent with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for stored credentials and sensitive data
  • Row-level security in our database ensuring tenant isolation
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only

6. Data Retention

Receipt images and extracted data are retained for the duration of the employer's service agreement plus 90 days, after which they are permanently deleted. Phone numbers are removed from our system promptly upon opt-out or employer request.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out — Reply STOP to any message at any time
  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate information

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@phoenixaiconsulting.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by employees of our business clients and is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
